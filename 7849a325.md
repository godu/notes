# Commutative diagram in latex

$$
\begin{equation}\begin{CD}
S^{{\mathcal{W}}_\Lambda}\otimes T   @>j>>   T\\
@VVV                                    @VV{End P}V\\
(S\otimes T)/I                  @=      (Z\otimes T)/J
\end{CD}\end{equation}
